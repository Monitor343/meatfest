The Special Details

Location: 4631 Olson Dr  Eau Claire, Wisconsin

Date: Saturday October 5th 2024

Times: I will be awake around 3:00am to get the smoker going for the brisket.

For most participants, you can begin to arrive at 8:00am

For those cooking meats on site, Let me know how much time you think you will need to complete your cook. If you need to start before 8:00am, we will make arrangements.

My goal is to have all foods ready to be served at 4:30pm

If you would like to more easily coordinate before fest day, use the contact form to request access to my MeatFest Discord channel so we can chat.

Weather: MeatFest '024 will happen rain or shine. I will have plenty of covered space in the event of rain. That same cover will also be available for shade if it is bright and sunny.

Participate in the Festival Of Meat!

Anyone is free and encouraged in participating in the Festival of Meat!.

Craft your own Meat Creation!

Anyone who wants to take on a meat project to share with the festival is more than welcome to. I would love for you to join me in the morning to smoke/cook some fantastic meat while we talk shop.

Anyone who would like to participate is welcome to bring thier grill/smoker/pit to my place so we can cook together. If you are interested in this, I can facilitate the transport of your grill/smoker/pit to my house for the day. Feel free to reachout to me using the form at the bottom of this page and we can get somthing planned.

Assist with Food Preperation

Getting everything ready for all the great people can be quite a task. We welcome anyone who wants to come early and assist with any other food and event prep.

A Little Thank You

For anyone who wants to come early to cook or otherwise assist, I will be providing a breakfast of pancakes, bacon, sausages, and OJ as a way to say thank you for your assistance.

Allergies

There will be attendees who have various food restrictions. Below is a list of allergies that I have been made aware of. For anyone bringing any dish, I ask that you bring a list of the allergen from the list below that are present in your dish.

Don't feel like you can't bring a dish if it contains one of the allergens below. If you can substitute ingredients to avoid a listed allergens without affecting the dish or creating a bunch of additional work; I am sure those with these allergies will appreciate it.

The goal is to make it easy for those with these allergies to make informed decissions about what is available to them to eat. I will be making little placards with any present allergens to accompany the dish on the table.

  • Corn: Whole Kernel and Other
  • Grapes
  • Gluten
  • Paprika
  • Pineapple
  • Tree Nuts
  • Wheat
